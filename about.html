About

This is a Project made by First-year Engineering Students of Pune Institute of Computer Technology.

This Project is done for Project Based Learning Subject which has an objective to motivate students to learn by working in group cooperatively to solve a problem, Project-based Learning(PBL) is a student centric pedagogy that involves a dynamic classroom approach in which it is believed that students acquire a deeper knowledge through active exploration of real world challenges and problems. Students learn about a subject by working for an extended period of time to investigate and respond to a complex question, challenge or a problem.
